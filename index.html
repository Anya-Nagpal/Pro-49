Interesting Facts About A Chameleon

  • Chameleon's are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing color is the most important part of communication among them.
  • They have tails which helps them wrap around the tree branches.

Interesting Facts About A Pigeon

  • Pigeons are complex and intelligent.
  • They have outstanding navigational abilities.
  • They also have amazing hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About A Camel

  • Camels are usually found in desserts.
  • They can live without drinking water for a long time.
  • They can move really fast in sand because of their specially designed feet.
